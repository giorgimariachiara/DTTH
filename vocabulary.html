The Vocabulary of Mafia

This graph shows the network analysis of the book. Besides showing which are the relationships between the different characters, it highlights the position of each of them in the crime case and so in the Mafia setting. In particular: green is for those that stand for the justice and the truth, red is for the ones that are part of the criminal organization and grey for those that have chosen the code of silence.
